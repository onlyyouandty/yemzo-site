Our services

Get to know what we do

Clearing and Forwarding

Our integrated services will ensure that the ever-changing requirements of the relevant government agencies are satisfied at the port of origin for your consignment therefore avoiding delays at the port of discharge.

Customs Clearance

We have extensive experience of dealing with international procedures and customs clearance procedures in Nigeria . We have excellent relations with port authorities in Nigeria , ensuring hassle free customs clearance. We render the FAST TRACK and POST CLEARANCE SERVICES to firms who have such licenses from the Nigerian Customs Service

Transportation and Warehousing

Upon arrival at the port of discharge, our custom clearance service will ensure that cargo is handled in the most efficient manner, discharged and delivered without delay ensuring the safety of cargo. We have access to private warehouses in key ports in Nigeria and West Africa. We provide secure and private warehousing at minimal rates.

Local Knowledge

Yemzo Freight handles cargo efficiently due to the in-depth local knowledge of our specialists. We act as the importer or exporter's agent in Nigeria while providing our full range of services to deliver clients consignment.
