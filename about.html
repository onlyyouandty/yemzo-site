Our history

Yemzo Frieght is a licenced customs agency by the federal government of Nigeria through the Nigeria customs service. We have provided clients with innovative solutions in complete End-to-End (Door to Door) delivery of air freight, sea freight(containerised cargo), Ro-Ro and project consignments. Our services range from freight forwarding, customs asycuda Direct Trade Input operator. We are providing port services to a wide range of individuals and companies involved in manufacturing, distribution, agro sciences, retailing, auto sale, construction, the power sector, oil and gas and other sectors. We provide freight solutions at all ports (sea ports and airports) in Nigeria.

We maintain strong relationships with some of the world's best shipping lines, Nigerian government agencies, port authorities. We work diligently to ensure our client's needs come first. Our clients recommend us for our, reliability, integrity and professionalism.
